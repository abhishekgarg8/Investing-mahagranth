6. [Peter Lynch on Making Money in the U.S. Stock Market - MOI Global transcript of 1994 National Press Club lecture](https://moiglobal.com/peter-lynch-1994/) - Secondary transcript of a public Lynch speech; strong for macro skepticism, business facts, and individual-investor encouragement.

8. [Interview With Peter Lynch - PBS Frontline](https://www.pbs.org/wgbh/pages/frontline/shows/betting/pros/lynch.html) - Public interview on Magellan, tenbaggers, and investor behavior; useful for C/D/E tasks too.

13. [SEC press release: Fidelity gift matter](https://www.sec.gov/news/press/2008/2008-32.htm) - Primary regulatory source for the 2008 broker-gift settlement involving Fidelity and Lynch; important non-hagiographic context.
14. [The Compound and Friends Peter Lynch interview transcript - Podscripts](https://podscripts.co/podcasts/the-compound-and-friends/how-peter-lynch-became-the-greatest-fund-manager-of-all-time) - 2025 transcript of Josh Brown interview; useful for late-career comments on mistakes, individual investors, and market risk. Auto-transcript quality should be checked before quoting.
15. [Lynch Foundation note on 2025 Compound interview](https://thelynchfoundation.com/news-archive/peter-lynch-interview-news) - Official Lynch Foundation page confirming the October 2025 interview and framing it as lessons from Lynch's investing career.
